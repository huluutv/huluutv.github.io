How to Login to Your Hulu TV Account

Logging in to your Hulu account only takes a few moments. Make sure you have your registered email address and password ready before beginning.

  1. Open the Hulu website or launch the Hulu app.
  2. Select the Log In option.
  3. Enter your registered email address.
  4. Type your password carefully.
  5. Click the Sign In button.
  6. Your Hulu account dashboard will open immediately.

Common Hulu Login Issues

If you're unable to sign in, double-check your email and password, verify your internet connection, clear your browser cache, restart the Hulu app, or reset your password if necessary. Keeping the app updated also helps prevent login problems.
